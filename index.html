Shooters
Team 9 O'CLICK

We are 9 o'click, a creative duo passionate about capturing moments that matter. As a husband-and-wife team, we specialize in photography, videography, and video editing, bringing stories to life through our lens. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, we aim to deliver stunning visuals that leave a lasting impact. Whether it's events, portraits, or creative projects, we are dedicated to making every shot count. Let’s create something amazing together!

Ves "The Husband" - Videographer, Video Editor, Photographer and Assistant

Ves is a skilled videographer and video editor with a passion for transforming ideas into stunning visuals. With expertise in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Lightroom Classic, he brings creativity and precision to every project. Armed with professional gear, including a mirrorless camera, gimbal, wireless mic, and drone, Ves captures high-quality footage that stands out. His experience with OBS Studio also makes him versatile in live streaming and production setups. From cinematic storytelling to seamless edits, Ves ensures every frame leaves a lasting impact.

AyMi "The Wife" - Photographer, Photo Editor and Assistant

AyMi is a skilled photographer who works alongside Ves, capturing stunning portraits and landscapes when he’s busy. She expertly edits all images in Photoshop and Lightroom Classic, ensuring every shot is polished to perfection. With a keen eye for detail, she brings moments to life through her lens.
